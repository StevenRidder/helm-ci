# NoForeignLand — reciprocity outreach (draft)

> A real, sendable note proposing **non-commercial read access** in exchange for **giving
> back** (tracks + contributions + attribution). Tone: peer-to-peer, community-minded, no
> commercial pressure. Send **after** the give-back is live, so it's proof, not promise.
> Context: [noforeignland.md](noforeignland.md) · [../BUILD-PLAN-COMMUNITY-LLM.md](../BUILD-PLAN-COMMUNITY-LLM.md).

---

## Who to contact

- NFL contact / support / the founder via the site's contact form or community channels.
- Lead with a human, non-commercial introduction — they're a small, mission-driven community
  project, not a BD desk.

## What to have ready (attach / link)

- A one-paragraph "what Helm is."
- **Proof we already give back:** Helm pushing tracks to NFL (Workstream A1, live).
- A precise mock of **exactly what we'd display** from NFL and **how we'd attribute** it.
- The explicit promise: cached, attributed, non-commercial, revocable.

## Draft email

**Subject:** Helm × NoForeignLand — we already push tracks to you; could we display your places (non-commercial, attributed)?

Hi [name],

I'm Steve, a cruiser building **Helm** — an open, offline-first chartplotter that fuses
charts, satellite, weather, and routing into one screen. I'm a NoForeignLand user and a fan;
the place database you've all built is genuinely the best cruising resource out there, and I
know you fundraise to keep it free.

I want to do this the right way, so I'm reaching out before doing anything with your data.

**What Helm already does for NFL:** Helm users can push their own track to NoForeignLand
(using their own boat key), so they show up for friends — that's live today. We'd happily
encourage that, and we can mirror opted-in user contributions your way too.

**What I'd love to ask:** permission for **non-commercial** read access to display your
places (and optionally boats) inside Helm — **cached, fully attributed to NoForeignLand, and
revocable any time.** It would feed an honest "where should I go" view alongside open data
(OpenStreetMap/OpenSeaMap) and our users' own reviews. I'm not trying to copy or redistribute
your database — just surface it, credited, for sailors who are already your community.

I understand the place data is your moat and that redistribution is exactly what you protect,
so I'm glad to:

- keep it **non-commercial** (Helm's free/open tier),
- **attribute** NoForeignLand clearly wherever your data appears, with links back to you,
- **cache responsibly** and never hammer your backend,
- scope it however you're comfortable — even a small pilot,
- and **send value back**: tracks, opted-in contributions, and new users discovering NFL
  through Helm.

If a data-sharing arrangement (formal or informal) is something you'd consider, I'd love to
talk through terms — or hear what would make this comfortable for you. If it's not something
you want, no worries at all; I'll keep cheering you on and keep pushing tracks your way.

Thanks for everything you've built.

Fair winds,
Steve
[contact]

## Notes for the sender

- **Don't overpromise commercial terms.** Open non-commercial; if Helm later commercializes,
  reopen with explicit commercial terms (see [LEGAL.md](../LEGAL.md)).
- **Lead with the give-back already shipped** — reciprocity is the whole pitch.
- If they decline or go quiet: **nothing breaks.** "Where to go" runs on open + owned + RAG;
  the personal-experimental pull stays personal-only ([ADR-0005](../decisions/0005-community-places-overlay.md)).
- Keep a copy of any permission granted in the attribution/ToS register ([LEGAL.md](../LEGAL.md)).
