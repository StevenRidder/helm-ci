Formalities & check-in

Fiji is straightforward but procedural. Savusavu is a designated Port of Entry; do not stop elsewhere first. Arrive during office hours to avoid overtime charges.

1
Call ahead on VHF 16
Hail "Copra Shed" or "Waitui Marina" before entering Nakama Creek; they coordinate the officials.
2
Fly the Q flag · stay aboard
No one ashore until cleared by Health, Customs, Immigration & Biosecurity.
3
Health → Customs → Immigration → Biosecurity
Officials come to the boat at the marina. Have crew list, clearance from last port, and ship's papers ready.
4
Biosecurity inspection
Fresh meat, eggs, some produce may be confiscated. Declare honestly.

Anchorage & moorings
Holding: good All-round shelter 6–12 m Book moorings Cyclone hole

Nakama Creek is a near-landlocked tidal inlet — flat in any wind, which is why it's a classic cyclone-season refuge. Marina moorings fill in season; anchoring room is limited and the creek is narrow, so reserve ahead.
